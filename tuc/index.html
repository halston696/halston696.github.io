here are a few hard-coded versions of TwitterUICustomizer, for use with the Electra Toolkit.
they're intended to be used on arm64 devices running the Electra Toolkit, and the latest twitter app (7.15 (possible lower) at the time of writing this).

to install, extract your downloaded zip and place it's contents in "/bootstrap/Library/SBInject"

  • TwitterUICustomizerBlue.zip
  • TwitterUICustomizerBlueAltNightMode.zip
  • TwitterUICustomizerGreen.zip
  • TwitterUICustomizerGreenAltNightMode.zip
  • TwitterUICustomizerOrange.zip
  • TwitterUICustomizerOrangeAltNightMode.zip
  • TwitterUICustomizerPink.zip
  • TwitterUICustomizerPinkAltNightMode.zip
